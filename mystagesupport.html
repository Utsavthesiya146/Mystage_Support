About MyStage

MyStage is a social platform where users can post photos, videos and short updates while the community votes to highlight one "Main Star" per day. MyStage helps creators, users and local businesses get visibility and grow their audience.

Key Features

  • Daily Spotlight (Main Star)
  • Post images, videos and text
  • Community voting, likes and comments
  • Public profiles and discovery
  • Business promotion & organic reach

Data & Security (Short)

We store user accounts, posts, comments, likes and engagement analytics in Supabase. Data is used only to provide app features and to improve the platform. We do not sell personal information.

Privacy Policy (English)

Privacy Policy — MyStage

Last updated: December 2025

MyStage collects account information (name, email, profile photo), user-generated content (posts, comments, likes), usage & analytics data, and device identifiers. All data is securely stored in Supabase and is used to operate and improve the service.

We do not sell personal data. Data may be shared only to comply with law or to prevent abuse. Users can request access, correction or deletion of their data by contacting support.
