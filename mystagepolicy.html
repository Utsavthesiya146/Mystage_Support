Privacy Policy (English)

This Privacy Policy describes how MyStage ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, stores and shares your information when you use our mobile application and related services.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: name, email address, profile photo, bio.
  • User Content: posts, images, videos, comments, likes, votes.
  • Usage Data: in-app actions, engagement metrics and analytics.
  • Device & Technical Data: device type, operating system, IP address, device identifiers.
  • Support Information: messages you send to support and related communications.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account and profile.
  • To display and publish your content inside the app.
  • To provide voting, spotlight and discovery features.
  • To analyse and improve app performance and user experience.
  • To detect and prevent abuse, fraud or illegal activity.

3. Data Storage & Security

We store user data in Supabase cloud services and use security best practices (encrypted transport, secure credentials). While we strive to protect your data, no online service can be 100% secure.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. We may share information only when:

  • Required by law or legal process.
  • To prevent harm or fraud.
  • With service providers who process data on our behalf (e.g., hosting, analytics).

5. Third‑Party Services

We may use third‑party service providers (Supabase, analytics providers, crash reporting). These providers have their own policies — we recommend reviewing their privacy terms.

6. Your Rights

  • Access: You can request a copy of your data.
  • Correction: You may update or correct your profile information.
  • Deletion: You can request deletion of your account and personal data.
  • Contact: Email us at utsavthesiya3@gmail.com for any request.

7. Children

MyStage is intended for users aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has an account, contact us to have it removed.

8. Cookies & Tracking

We and our partners may use cookies or similar tracking technologies to provide and improve our services. These are used for analytics and feature functionality.

9. Changes to this Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will post the updated policy with a new effective date.
